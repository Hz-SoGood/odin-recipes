Cake

Description

A sweet, baked, breadlike food, made with or without shortening, and usually containing flour, sugar, baking powder or soda, eggs, and liquid flavoring

The invention of cake originated in ancient Egypt as round, flat, unleavened breads that were cooked on a hot stone. The evolution of baked goods continued over many centuries through the introduction of new ingredients and the invention of new techniques of baking.Oct 30, 2014

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • fat (usually butter)
  • sugar
  • salt
  • a form of liquid (usually milk)
  • leavening agents (such as baking soda)

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together
  2. Add liquid
  3. Bake in oven
  4. Let sit after baking to settle
